Finding a username

Usually, the display name of a user is their username. But if a user signed in e.g. via facebook, their display name might differ from their username. You can find the username of your own account at https://www.spotify.com/account/overview/.

Which data can be used?

You can access all public information of other users, i.e. only their public, non-collaborative playlists. Regarding your own user account, you can also access your private and collaborative playlists. For this, you have to give this app access to them. You will be prompted to grant access if you run your first analysis.

Find common songs in two playlists

Enter the name of two playlists and the username of the respective owner.









Find a song in a user's playlists

Enter the link to a song by dragging and dropping the song into the input field. The link starts with "https://open.spotify.com/track/" followed by an ID.
